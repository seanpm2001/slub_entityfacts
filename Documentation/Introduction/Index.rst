What does it do?
----------------

The following document holds information about the installation of the TYPO3 SLUB_EntityFacts extension. In case of further questions please contact the release management.

This TYPO3 extension is primarily meant to show additional facts of historical personalities or places. Those facts are taken from the DNB (Deutsche Nationalbibliothek).
